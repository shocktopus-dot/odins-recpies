2 Mins Maggi for a rainy day

Hello Foodies! Today I will be sharing a quick, savory instant noodles recipe for a rainy day. It will only take 2 minn of your time and will fill your stomach for the rest off the night

Ingredients

  • Maggi packet - one oil - 1 tbsp minced garlic - 2 cloves chopped onions - 1 cup chopped green chilli - 2 red chilli powder - 1tsp chopped ccapsicum - 1/2 cup chopped carrot - 1/2 cup corn - 1/2 cup soya sauce - 1 tbsp vinnegar - 1/2 tbsp

    • Instructions

      add oil, garlic and chilli to the pan add onionns and fry them until golden brown add the vegatables and cover it with a lid add chilli powder and maggi masala add vinegar and soysauce add 1 cup of water add noodles and wait until it comes to boil finally add some freshly chopped onions to the bowl

    Your maggi is ready!
